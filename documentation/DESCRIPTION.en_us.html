<p><strong>Elms Sans</strong> is a utilitarian geometric sans serif built for clarity, resilience, and warmth.</p>


<p>Inspired by the strength and structure of early geometric typefaces, Elms Sans was designed to perform across a wide range of applications—from user interfaces and signage to editorial design and identity systems. It embraces the rationality of geometric forms, but introduces enough openness and warmth to stay approachable in both large and small sizes.</p>


<p>The name references the elm tree—known for its durability and graceful form—echoing the typeface’s balance of function and visual harmony.</p>


<p>Elms Sans is available in eight weights from Thin to Heavy. Each style is carefully spaced and kerned to ensure versatility across modern digital and print environments.</p>


<p>Designed by Amarachi Nwauwa.</p>
